Tap is a modern Ableton Live controller designed for both **iPhone and iPad**.  
Whether you're launching clips, shaping sounds, sequencing drums or tweaking devices, Tap gives you a fast, clean and beautifully responsive interface in a small form factor.

## Why an Ableton Live Controller App?
Why turn two knobs when you have ten fingers and could tweak up to 8 macros at a time and still have two fingers left for mixing? Hardware controllers have their charm, but at project7III we focus on the future. A touchscreen is simply the most flexible and precise tool for adjusting parameters, launching clips, sequencing, and mixing.  
That's why we created Tap: the most performance-dense Ableton Live controller in the world. If you have a few bucks to spare and an iOS device, you're ready to upgrade your workflow.  
  
Here are some of the reasons why you should consider Tap:
- **Works on both iPhone and iPad**  
- **Plug-and-play** with Ableton Live via USB (Mac) or WiFi (Mac & Windows)
- **Beautiful, clean interface** designed for performance
- **Three powerful views**: Device, Clips, Mixer
- **Awesome MIDI sequencers, keyboards and pads**
- **Universal MIDI CC mode** for controlling any instrument or app
- **Adaptive layouts** for portrait and landscape on iPad

### Device View
- Different MIDI keyboards & pads  
- Drum and melodic step sequencer  
- Devices & banks  
- 8 encoders per bank
- Modulation tools and powerful note editing  

Designed for quick sound design, live modulation, and fast sequencing.

### Clips View
- Launch, record, append, copy & duplicate clips  
- Trigger and duplicate scenes  
- Access devices without switching to another screen  
- Designed for fast performance workflows 

### Mixer View
- Volume, sends, pan, solo, and mute layout  
- Integrated clips panel

### Custom Encoders
- Control any MIDI-compatible device or software with fully customizable encoders.  
- Perfect for synths, drum machines, external apps, and master effects parameters.

### Connectivity
- **USB** (macOS)
- **WiFi** (macOS & Windows)
- No server or Max for Live device required. Simply download the MIDI remote script and put it in the correct folder. More information [here](/tap/manual/#2-1-add-the-tap-midi-remote-script).

## FAQ

### Does Tap work with Ableton Live on Mac and Windows?
Yes. Connecting via USB works on macOS. Connecting via WiFi works on both macOS and Windows.

### Can Tap replace a hardware controller like Push?
For many workflows and most musicians, yes.  
Tap includes clip launching, device editing, mixing and a powerful step sequencer in a mobile form factor.

### Does Tap also work on iPhone?
Yes. Tap is one of the few fully featured iPhone Ableton Live controllers. Basically a Push the size of an iPhone.  

### Does Tap work on Android?
Not yet, sorry.

### Do I need additional software?
You only need to install the Tap MIDI Remote Script once. After that, Tap connects automatically.

### Does Tap support MPE or Aftertouch?
Not yet. Standard MIDI features are supported, like velocity, modulation and pitch bend.

### Will Tap make my music sound better?
Unfortunately not. But you will be able to make more crappy music in less time, and gain the power to entertain (or terrify) crowds for hours, using only your laptop and your iPad.
